Log4j in relation to HashiCorp Products

Summary:

HashiCorp products and services have no known exposure to the Apache Log4j 2 security issue (CVE-2021-44228) at this time

Bulletin Link:

HCSEC-2021-32 Apache Log4j 2

HashiCopr Consul 1.11 GA:

HashiCorp Consul 1.11 adds important new features: multi-tenancy with administrative partitions, new installation-and-amangement Consul Kubernetes CLS, and Vault integration on Consul Kubernetes.

Terraform 1.1 Improved Refactoring and Cloud CLI:

Announcing HashiCorp Vault 1.9:
